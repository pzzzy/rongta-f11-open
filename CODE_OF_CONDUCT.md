# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include empathy, respectful feedback, accepting responsibility, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, publishing private information, and conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. Report unacceptable behavior privately through the repository owner's GitHub contact channels. Reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly, with respect for the reporter's privacy and safety.
